# LaunchKit

LaunchKit generates **Google Ads headlines and copy**, **AI-generated ad images**, and your **AI visibility score**—all from your website URL.

Drop your website URL and get:
- **Headlines & copy** — Short and long headlines plus descriptions, optimized for Google Ads character limits. Copy directly into your campaigns.
- **Generated ad images** — AI-generated images in landscape, square, and portrait. On-brand and ready to download.
- **AI visibility score** — See how well you show up in AI search (ChatGPT, Perplexity, etc.). Get a score, actionable recommendations, and blog topic ideas.

## Getting started

  * Run `mix setup` to install and setup dependencies
  * Start Phoenix endpoint with `mix phx.server` or inside IEx with `iex -S mix phx.server`

Visit [`localhost:4000`](http://localhost:4000) from your browser.
